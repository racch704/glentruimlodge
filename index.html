Our Ecopods

Welcome to our delightful ecopods, Muckle Meadow and Cozy Coorie, in the Cairngorms National Park. Perfect for two people, our ecopods offer a peaceful retreat for outdoor enthusiasts and come fully equipped with kitchenette and ensuite shower-room. You can also enjoy fast wifi and streaming TVs. Our ecopods offer stunning views and a tranquil retreat after exploring the national park. Perfect for if you're visiting the area or just passing through.

GET TO US

- BY CAR

We are located about 1 mile from the A9, approximately one hour from both Perth and Inverness. We are ideally situated as a stopover on the way to the Isle of Skye.

- BY BICYCLE

National Cycle Route 7 runs beside the A9 just 1 mile away. We are 4 miles south of Newtonmore and about 9 miles from Dalwhinnie. You can store your bicycles securely overnight.

- BY TRAIN

The nearest train station is Newtonmore, which is about 4 miles away. Kingussie train station is only 6 miles away and is served by many more trains. If you are arriving by train, we can usually arrange in advance to collect you from the station for a modest fee.
