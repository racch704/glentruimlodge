Embrace the Space: Our Muckle Meadow Ecopod

Discover the breathtaking beauty of the Scottish Highlands from your own private sanctuary. Our Muckle Meadow ecopod ('muckle' meaning 'big' in Scots) sits perched atop our meadow, offering panoramic views and an abundance of wildlife. Watch red squirrels scamper, spot majestic deer, and keep an eye out for elusive wildcats â€“ all from the comfort of your cozy retreat.

Built in 2023, our spacious ecopod is a haven of modern comfort amidst the wild landscape. Prepare simple meals in the fully equipped kitchenette and refresh in your private ensuite bathroom. Step outside onot the large deck and immerse yourself in the vast scenery, surrounded by the sounds of nature.

The ecopod is approximately 21 square metres. It has double-opening glass doors. It also has two opening windows, one to the side and one to the rear.
