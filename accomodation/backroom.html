Back Double Room with Shared Bathroom

Our double room offers garden and countryside views and includes a continental breakfast. It shares a modern bathroom with another room and provides shared access to a spacious kitchen, where you can cook and eat, as well as our sunroom, offering peaceful relaxation with views of the grounds.

Feel free to utilise the kitchen facilities, and we've allocated a shelf in both the fridge and freezer for your convenience. We kindly request that you clean up after cooking and dining. Please keep in mind that access to other bedrooms is restricted, and we kindly ask that you refrain from entering them. It's important to note that both the kitchen and sunroom are shared spaces with our family and fellow guests.
