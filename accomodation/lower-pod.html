Cozy Coorie

Enjoy our quiet self-catering ensuite ecopod at Glentruim, in the heart of the Cairngorms National Park. Our ecopod is in a quiet corner of our smallholding, 30m from our cottage, among lovely beech trees. During your visit, you are likely to see red squirrels, deer, birds of prey, and numerous other birds in the vicinity. If you are exceptionally lucky, you may even catch sight of elusive and rare Scottish Wildcat, osprey and golden eagles. Hens and ducks roam free around the garden.

Built in 2019, our ecopod sleeps two and offers a peaceful retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Inside the ecopod, you'll find a fully equipped kitchenette, complete with a microwave, fridge and kettle, as well as an outdoor gas BBQ. The ensuite toilet and shower room is equipped with towels, toilet paper and a heated towel rail.
