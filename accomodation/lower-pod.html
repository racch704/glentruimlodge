Cozy Coorie Ecopod

Nestled in the heart of the Cairngorms National Park, our secluded ecopod offers a tranquil retreat for couples seeking a peaceful getaway. Immerse yourself in the beauty of the Scottish Highlands, surrounded by beech trees and the sounds of nature. Your Cozy Coorie (coorie meaning 'cuddle' in Scots) awaits, a private haven in the corner of our smallholding. Watch for red squirrels, deer, and birds of prey from your doorstep. If you're lucky, you might even glimpse a rare Scottish Wildcat or soaring osprey. Our ecopod, built in 2019, is a haven of comfort for two. Enjoy a fully equipped kitchenette, perfect for simple meals, and a private ensuite bathroom. Step outside for a delicious BBQ amidst the stunning scenery. The exopod is approximately 15 square metres. It has a wooden floor and double-opening glass doors. It also has an opening window with a wooden blind at the rear.

Ready to unwind and reconnect with nature? Book your Cozy Coorie escape today or contact us to learn more.
