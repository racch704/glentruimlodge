Glentruim

Glentruim Homestay B&B and Ecopods rests at the end of a private track in the heart of the Cairngorms National Park. Situated amidst stunning natural beauty, you will enjoy spectacular views of the surrounding hills and glens. During your stay, you will likely encounter red squirrels and deer roaming the grounds, and you will see a variety of birds of prey, as well as the more abundant pheasants and grouse. If you are exceptionally fortunate, you may even catch sight of the elusive and highly endangered Scottish Wildcat, which is native to this area.

Ecopods

Our modern, well-equipped self-catering ecopods offer comfortable accommodations for couples. Each ecopod features a kitchenette, full shower room, central heating, fast Wi-Fi, and a smart TV.

Bed & Breakfast

Our B&B is cozy and welcoming. You can relax at the table in the large, inviting kitchen, which is always kept warm by the AGA cooker, or in our spectacular sunroom, which has comfortable seating and panoramic windows overlooking the grounds. Fast, reliable Wi-Fi is available throughout the house.

Local Area

Glentruim Homestay B&B and Ecopods is just 200 meters from the River Spey, where you can fish with a locally purchased permit. It is also the ideal location to experience outdoor sporting activities such as skiing, sailing, hillwalking, climbing, and mountain biking.
